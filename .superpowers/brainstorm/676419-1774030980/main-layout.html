Key Design Elements

  • Dynamic Color: Material You theming - accent colors adapt to wallpaper
  • Bottom Navigation: 4 tabs - Home, Servers, Setup, Settings
  • Compact Header: Status indicator in top bar (colored dot + text)
  • Flat List Items: Clean rows with subtle dividers, no heavy cards
  • Rounded Containers: 12-16px radius for grouped content
  • Section Headers: Uppercase, small, muted color labels
