Recommendation: Option 3 (Material You Hybrid)

The hybrid design combines the best aspects:

  • Precision: Subtle crosshairs help with exact positioning
  • Visibility: Outer glow ensures cursor is visible on any background

  • Contrast: White border around center dot provides visibility
  • Performance: No continuous animations (optional pulsing glow on tap)
